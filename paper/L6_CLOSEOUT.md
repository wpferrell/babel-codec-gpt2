straight through to the output unchanged, and THAT is what looked like steering. When we measure the echo
directly (inject at the very last layer, where nothing can compute on it) it accounts for essentially the
whole effect; the genuine computed part is within the noise. So the last L4 anomaly DISSOLVES: no new
capability, manifold-bound stands, and the L4 headline result is untouched (its own control always held).

BOTTOM LINE: the 5.3% is DIFFUSE and mostly word-less (favorite bet lost, more informative than a win); the
rung is read-only at every magnitude AND has a concentrated downstream listener (one bet hit, one lost); the
last L4 anomaly was read-out echo (favorite bet lost). Three favorite bets lost, two hit; every loss is a
